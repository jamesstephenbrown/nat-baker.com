Kia ora!


I’m a writer living in Auckland, N.Z.

You can read my short stories and personal essays at HER, Bravado, Art+Money (USA), Headland and Takahe.

Most recently I’ve been published at Newsroom and Milly Magazine and was short-listed for the Surrey Hotel-Newsroom Award (2020 edition).

In 2021, I won the Takahe Short Story Prize for ‘Last Bird Island’.

My short story ‘One night with you’ is forthcoming in Brief.

I am currently querying my debut short story collection ‘Goodbye, Steve’ (14 stories) and working on my first novel.

I hold a Bachelor of Arts (Hons) in Anthropology; and a Masters in Creative Writing (MCW) from the University of Auckland.
